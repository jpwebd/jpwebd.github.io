chocolate chip cookies

You will need

  • 115 g of unsalted butter
  • 100 g of light brown sugar
  • 100 g of granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp of vanilla
  • 185 g of flour
  • 7 g of cornstarch
  • 240 g chocolate chips
  • 1/2 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp of salt

First, cream the softened butter and sugars by hand, and add the egg and vanilla.
Then, in a separate bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, cornstarch and salt.
Add gradually to the butter mixture and stir until well blended.
After that, add the chocolate chips and stir.
Shape the cookies in small balls and place them on an oven-safe tray for 45 minutes in the fridge.
Preheat the oven on 160 degrees and bake them for 15-17 minutes until the edges turn slightly brown.
